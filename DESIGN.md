# STUDYFOLLOW Design System

## Brand direction
STUDYFOLLOW uses a cinematic dark-academic interface with glassmorphism surfaces, neon cyan and violet highlights, and deep navy backgrounds. The goal is to feel focused, premium, and motivating without becoming visually chaotic.

## Components
- Buttons use a blue-cyan glow gradient for primary actions.
- Cards use blurred dark surfaces with soft borders and luminous shadows.
- Inputs are dark translucent fields with subtle focus rings.
- Status badges use tinted surfaces matching their semantic color.
